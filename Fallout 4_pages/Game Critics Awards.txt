The Game Critics Awards are a set of annual awards held after the E3 video game conference since 1998. The awards are given to products displayed at E3 with the title Best of E3 of their category. The nominees and winners of the awards are chosen by individual judges representing 35 (as of 2011) major North American media outlets. The awards are given independently of the Expo's organizers.


== Award winners ==
Note on "all systems" as platform: Because games shown at E3 are still in development, the platforms for which the game is published might still be in question.
